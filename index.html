“Peaky Blinders: The Rise of the Shelby Empire in Post-War Birmingham”

Peaky Blinders is a British crime drama television series created by Steven Knight that aired its first episode on September 12, 2013, on BBC Two. The show is set in post-World War I Birmingham, England, and centers around the Shelby family, a notorious gang known as the Peaky Blinders, who rise to prominence through crime, politics, and business. The show has gained international acclaim for its dark, gritty atmosphere, compelling characters, and historical context, blending fiction with real historical events and figures.
